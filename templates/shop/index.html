Frequently Asked Questions

For the most accurate and detailed birth chart readings, your exact birth time is essential. However, we can still provide valuable insights with just your birth date and location, though some specific details like your rising sign and house placements may not be as precise.

A natal (birth) chart reading reveals your inherent personality traits, strengths, challenges, and life path based on the planetary positions at your birth. A transit reading examines how current and upcoming planetary movements interact with your birth chart, highlighting timing for specific life events and opportunities.

Astrology doesn't predict exact events but rather highlights patterns, tendencies, and optimal timing. It shows potential energy patterns and how they might manifest. Free will always plays a crucial role in how these energies express themselves in your life. We focus on empowerment through awareness rather than deterministic predictions.

Come with specific questions or areas of life you'd like to explore. Having a focus helps us provide more targeted guidance. Also, approach the reading with an open mind and be willing to participate in the conversation. Many clients find it helpful to record the session (with permission) or take notes to review later.
