Astrology is not about predicting exact events but rather about understanding patterns and potentials. It reveals the energies and influences at play in your life, helping you make more informed choices. Free will always plays a crucial role in how these energies manifest in your life. Think of astrology as a cosmic weather forecast - it can tell you if it might rain, but you decide whether to carry an umbrella.

Western astrology (which we primarily use) is based on the tropical zodiac, which follows the seasons. Vedic astrology uses the sidereal zodiac, which tracks the actual positions of constellations. This creates a difference of about 23 degrees between the two systems. Western astrology often focuses more on psychological insights and personality, while Vedic astrology tends to be more predictive and fate-oriented. We can provide readings in either system based on your preference.
