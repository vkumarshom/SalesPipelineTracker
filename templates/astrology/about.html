The Seven Chakras in Astrology

Understanding the connection between energy centers and planetary influences

Root Chakra

Connected to Saturn, the Root Chakra grounds us and provides stability and security.

Sacral Chakra

Ruled by Mars and Venus, the Sacral Chakra governs creativity, emotion, and pleasure.

Solar Plexus

Influenced by the Sun, the Solar Plexus Chakra relates to personal power and self-confidence.

Heart Chakra

Connected to Venus, the Heart Chakra represents love, compassion, and harmony.

Throat Chakra

Ruled by Mercury, the Throat Chakra facilitates communication and self-expression.

Third Eye Chakra

Associated with Neptune and the Moon, the Third Eye governs intuition and insight.

Crown Chakra

Connected to Uranus and Jupiter, the Crown Chakra represents spiritual consciousness and enlightenment.
